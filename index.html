About ICAPIE DTU

The International Conference on Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (ICAPIE) is aimed to promote synergetic partnership between academia and industry and also to show case the growth of cutting edge technology developed in recent past in the areas of Production and Industrial Engineering. ICAPIE assembles the best talents in the academia & research institutions, industry experts, management professionals, engineers and executives to collaborate and address the current challenges in Production and Industrial Engineering, to seek new windows for discovery and exploration. It is an invitation to all scientist, engineers and technologist to create new paradigm using the new knowledge, vision and scientific intellect. The organisers are grateful to all authors and participants who have made significant contributions in the form of their papers. We also like to thank the reviewers for their comments and suggestions which helped in improving the quality of the papers. We hope that ICAPIE 2021 conference also will prove to be a great success with the academia, researchers and engineers.

ABOUT CAPIER DTU

CAPIER, Centre for Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering Research was established on 8 April 2015. We know CAPIER for organizing expert lectures, workshops, and industry-institute interactions. CAPIER invites B.Tech. & M.Tech. students for an internship during summer and winter holidays. CAPIER also organizes a visit to industries for motivating the students towards advanced technology. CAPIER holds a name in the institution for supervising many excellent projects for B.Tech., M.Tech. and at the level of Ph.D. CAPIER also support IJAPIE, an international journal in production and industrial engineering. CAPIER started organizing an international conference in the name of ICAPIE. The first international conference ICAPIE-2016 was a magnificent success. The second international conference ICAPIE ’17 was in partnership with many institutions and made a name of its own. The proceedings of the conference were greatly appreciated. CAPIER also supports faculty development programs and staff development programs. AIM-2015 and SDTSMT-2015 were successful with the sponsorship of Teqip-II. It marked AMOM 2017 a two weeks program as best FDP by the participants. Industry Institution Interactions were held regularly. It organized a talk on ROBO and practice of Robotics and Automation. CAPIER at Delhi Technological University is getting an excellent name and growing with experienced faculty and budding researchers.

FAQs

  • Can I register at the conference on the spot?

    Yes, the provision will be made at the registration desk on-site for last-minute attendees wishing to participate in ICAPIE DTU.

  • What is included in my registration fee?

    A kit of our conference items including the abstract book, proceedings, brochure, schedule of the conference along with the certificate of presentation of paper will be provided to all registered individuals. Attendees and participants are advised to make their own arrangements prior to their arrival.

  • How can I see a list of who is attending? Will speaker presentations be available after the event?

    A full participant list will be available on-site at the registration desk before the Conference commences. A list of the keynote speakers will be provided in the schedule that will be sent via email on successful registration. The schedule will also be provided to all the speakers and attendees on arrival. However, speaker presentations will not be available after the conference.

  • I am an overseas participant and need a letter of invitation before I can receive my visa. What is the procedure?

    CAPIER DTU is willing to issue letters of invitations (under terms and conditions) to those who require it. For requests to acquire one, please email us at icapie.dtu@gmail.com.

  • Can I register if I’m not associated with any institution/agency?

    Yes. CAPIER DTU encourages students, independent researchers, and freelancers to participate in ICAPIE. Affiliation with any institution, company, or agency is not a requirement.

  • Will I receive an itinerary?

    Yes. The details will be posted on the official ICAPIE DTU site and on all the social media handles as they are confirmed. A full conference schedule, including conference sessions, will be available on our website soon.

  • Do I have to be present at the conference to attend? Can I participate in the conference online?

    No, one may simply choose to attend the conference without presenting. However, it should be noted that the registration process and the charges for attending and for presenting at the conference are different. ICAPIE DTU will be held in an offline mode so online participation will not be possible.

  • May I submit more than one research paper?

    Yes, more than one research paper by the same participant is allowed. Although it should be noted that they will be counted as separate entries to the conference and charged accordingly.

  • Will there be A/V equipment at the conference venue, and what do I need to bring in order to use it?

    Yes, A/V equipment will be made available. To avail the same, please bring your presentations in a USB flash drive or a hard drive. It is advisable to check with the system coordinators once on site before the final presentation in order to prevent any last-minute technical issues.

  • Will I receive a certificate of attendance?

    All participants at ICAPIE DTU will be awarded a certificate of attendance. In case an attendee is not presenting, he/she will be provided a certificate on a payment basis, on request, subject to availability. For any other query, kindly email us at icapie.dtu@gmail.com
